How Sanvi Organics Works

A simple process that ensures quality, transparency, and fair pricing for everyone involved.

1

Farmers List Products

Local organic farmers list their fresh produce on our platform with their desired prices.

2

We Create QR Labels

Each product gets a unique QR code showing its journey, pricing, and farm details.

3

You Shop & Scan

Browse products, scan QR codes to verify pricing and source, and place your order.

4

Direct Delivery

Products are delivered fresh from farms to your doorstep, maintaining the cold chain.

QR Transparency: Know What You Pay For

Our unique QR labeling system puts the power back in your hands. Each product comes with a QR code that reveals:

  • The farm's location and farming practices
  • Harvest date and journey timeline
  • Original price paid to the farmer
  • Final price to you with breakdown
  • Certifications and quality testing results

No hidden fees, no unfair markups, just transparent pricing that's fair to both farmers and consumers.
